How to Play

Classic game of memory. Flip over two cards to reveal the fruit behind them. If the two cards match, you score points! If not, you lose a turn.

* One match = 100 points/1 turn
* Two matches in a row = 250 points/2 turns
* Three matches in a row = 500 points/3 turns

The game is over when you match all the cards or run out of turns!
